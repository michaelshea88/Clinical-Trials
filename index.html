Mining ClinicalTrials.gov

Data mining 40,000+ oncology studies on ClinicalTrials.gov

ClinicalTrials.gov is a government-funded registry of over 200,000 clinical trials. Since 2007, virtually all major studies of drugs and biologics have been required by law to be registered on the site.

The site is maintained by the National Institutes of Health and lists studies conducted in all 50 states and 192 countries. Each trial page contains a detailed description of the conditions, interventions and outcomes being studied, as well as the study design, enrollment and funding sources.
